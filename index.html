About our name

The Recursive Human refers to the human's ability to think about thinking*.

Awareness of how we and others think is the starting point for all projects at Recursive Human Ltd.

*Recursion refers to a process that makes calls to itself and is an important concept at the base of many complex phenomena including language, human governance, agentic ai and biological systems.
